What to expect

What happens in the first session.

  1. Arrive, check in, and complete a brief safety screen
  2. Name what you want support with
  3. Learn one simple breath or nervous system concept
  4. Move through a guided breathflow at your pace
  5. Ground, integrate, and leave with a simple practice
Important

Deeper breathwork may not be suitable for everyone. If you have significant medical or mental health conditions, pregnancy, seizure history, heart concerns, or recent surgery, please check with a qualified professional first.
