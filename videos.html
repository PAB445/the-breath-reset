The 90-second coherent breath reset.

Coherent breathing gently nudges your nervous system toward balance. Equal, unhurried breaths — in for five, out for five — signal safety to the body.

  • Best for: feeling switched on, wired, or tense.
  • How long: ninety seconds, nine slow breaths.
  • How to use it: watch the orb, match your breath, let the pace lead.

When you finish, take one normal breath and notice what feels different. Save it for when you need to come back to yourself.

Extended exhale breath

For when your mind feels scattered

Box breathing

Equal counts with steady holds bring structure and focus. The soft square traces each phase, so the pauses feel deliberate, not frozen.
